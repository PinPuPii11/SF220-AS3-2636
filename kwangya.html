Getting to know KWANGYA

KWANGYA

Virtual world

KWANGYA (Hangul: 광야, Gwang-ya) is a word in Korean meaning "wilderness". Wilderness means the wilds, waste, jungle or desert. KWANGYA is a world where the avatars live.
